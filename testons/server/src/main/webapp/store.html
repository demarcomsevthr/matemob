Therapy Reminder App

Presentation

I have developed this app for mobile devices with the purpose to offer the possibility to send an email later in the future.

This app will allow you to send an email at your personal inbox in the exact moment you want to receive it.

You will have to login with your Google account and then you will be able to send emails to your Gmail inbox at any time in the future.

All the process of authentication is delegated to the Google infrastructure. This ensures that all your data will be kept safe and protected.

In next releases you will be able to send emails recoursively every certain time.

And coming soon... even SMS!
